About Us


Our website allows users to shop for grocery store products. By clicking on the Sign Up or Sign In button the user will be navigated to the registration and sign in page. From there the user can sign in to their account or register an account. To begin shopping the user must click on the start shopping button and they will be sent to the shopping page. The user may use the search bar to find specific items and they can use the filter feature to sort the products by the given options. From there they can add their desired items to their wishlist or cart by using the add buttons in each items box. To view their wishlist all the user has to do is click on the wishlish button on the tool bar and to view their cart they have to click on the cart button. Once the user is done shopping they must click on the cart button and then use the checkout button to be sent to the checkout page to pay for their groceries. The user will then be prompted to choose how they will pay and enter the number on their card. Finally the user may make the purchase and finalize their shopping. Thanks for visiting our website!
