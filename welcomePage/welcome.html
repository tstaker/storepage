About Us


Our website allows users to shop for grocery store products. The user may use the search bar to find specific items and they can use the filter feature to sort the products by the given options. From there they can add their desired items to their wishlist or cart by using the add buttons in each items box. To view their wishlist all the user has to do is click on the wishlish button on the tool bar and for their cart they have to click on the checkout button and then the load cart button (there is still more to be updated). Thanks for visiting our website!
